Great White Shark

Great White Sharks can detect a drop of blood in 25 gallons of water up to 3 miles away.

  • Scientific Name: Carcharodon Carcharias
  • Average Length: 15 feet
  • Average Weight: 5000lbs
  • Speed: 25mph

Great Whites are found in coastal waters. With up to 300 teeth, they are the largest sea predators and feed on sea lions, seals, and sea turtles.
